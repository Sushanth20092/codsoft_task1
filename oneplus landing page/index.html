OnePlus 8

The OnePlus 8 offers an immersive 90Hz Fluid Display, powerful Snapdragon 865 chipset, and an elegant design with a stunning AMOLED display. It's designed for users who want the best performance and the sleekest look in their smartphone.

Display

6.55-inch AMOLED display with 90Hz refresh rate for ultra-smooth visuals.

Performance

Powered by the Qualcomm Snapdragon 865 with 5G support for lightning-fast speeds.

Camera

48MP triple-camera system for stunning photos and videos, even in low light.

Processor

Qualcomm Snapdragon 865, 2840 MHz, Kryo 585, 64-bit.

Features

Fingerprint (under display, optical), accelerometer, gyro, proximity, compass.

Battery

Type: Li-Po 4300 mAh, Charging: 30W wired, 50% in 22 min.
